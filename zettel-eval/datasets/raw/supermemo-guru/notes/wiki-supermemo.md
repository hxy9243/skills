**SuperMemo** is a speed-learning application that helps you retain knowledge for life.

You can read more about SuperMemo in *[What is SuperMemo?](https://help.supermemo.org/wiki/SuperMemo)*, or just give it a test: [Download SuperMemo](https://supermemopedia.com/wiki/Download_SuperMemo).

The most advanced form of learning with SuperMemo is [incremental reading](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Incremental_reading "Incremental reading"). Versions of SuperMemo that support incremental reading:

- SuperMemo 16 for Windows ([Freeware](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/SuperMemo_Freeware "SuperMemo Freeware"))
- SuperMemo 19 for Windows ([commercial](https://supermemo.store/products/supermemo-19-for-windows))
