## Neural networks are color blind

The brain is a [concept network](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Concept_network "Concept network"). At the molecular level, it is hard to differentiate between networks belonging to people of different races.

The [conceptualization](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Conceptualization "Conceptualization") process that leads to [intelligence](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Intelligence "Intelligence") follows the same principles in all healthy individuals

## Discrimination affects intelligence

White supremacist love to claim their race is superior to other races. Most of all, they cherish their alleged intellectual superiority. One of their favorite racist tools is research on the average IQ of different races. [IQ is a dismal measure of intelligence](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/IQ_is_a_dismal_measure_of_intelligence "IQ is a dismal measure of intelligence"). [Well-schooled](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Well-schooled "Well-schooled") people do well on IQ tests, which have little to do with [intelligence](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Intelligence "Intelligence"). Intelligence is always domain dependent. In addition, IQ tests are culturally biased. You can construct a test that will favor women. Last but not least, long history of oppression and discrimination perpetuate some cultural myths that affect the development that affects IQ scores.

Next time you hear a claim of racial superiority, ask the claimant about the difference between black and white brains, or the difference between black and white neurons, or proteins involved in weaving neural networks. [Intelligence](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Intelligence "Intelligence") is best understood as the ability to [solve problems](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Problem_solving "Problem solving"), and the potential to develop highest intelligence between brains of different races is largely indistinguishable. The path to high intelligence is an obstacle course that makes sure that a vast majority of people never reach their maximum potential. We are limited in the cradle, in [daycare](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Daycare "Daycare"), at school, at work, and in various social contexts. It is not easy to stay perfectly healthy in the modern world. Assaults on intelligence are plenty. Otherwise, we would have a society of geniuses. Those assaults tend to be more dramatic for some races and this is why the average levels of adaptation may differ for different strata of the population.

We can have an Turing test for blackness, and the only differences we will be able to detect are those where there is a cultural bias in knowledge. Some of my black friends admit that they enjoy colorless life on the web, where discrimination is gone, and diversity is an advantage. An interesting offshoot of that color blindness shows up in [artificial intelligence](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Artificial_intelligence "Artificial intelligence") tools responsible for categorizing competence or suitability (e.g. in jobs, investments, etc.). If gender or color are eliminated from the input data, the discrimination evaporates, or only reflects natural preferences associated with gender or race.

Discrimination and intelligence live in a feedback loop. For centuries we discriminated against people of color and women. Discrimination becomes self-fulfilling prophecy by affecting self-esteem. Today, the main culprit is the discrimination against children. We deem children not intelligent enough. This discrimination leads to [compulsory schooling](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Compulsory_schooling "Compulsory schooling"), which is the worst offender in undermining intelligence in 2022. Coercive schooling is also a [violation of human rights](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Children%E2%80%99s_Freedom:_A_Human_Rights_Perspective "Children’s Freedom: A Human Rights Perspective").

Discrimination is one of key factors undermining global intelligence

## Neurodiversity is precious

As for genetic differences, in evolution, brains are optimized for the needs of the species in addition to those of the individual. This is why we cannot say a brain is an optimal problem solver from the point of view of an individual. We need different brains (neurodiversity), and each brain serves its own purpose in the population. If we modulate the levels of testosterone, we will modulate the strategies in problem solving. We need explorers who risk their lives, and those who patiently settle the new lands. Male and female brains differ and may employ different optimization criteria in maximizing adaptation.

If there is a different level of neurogenesis, we may have a different speed of adaptation that may be subject to stability-vs-accuracy trade-off. Too much neurogenesis would make brains effective in short-term only. Too little neurogenesis would lead to stagnation in adaptation. Again, there is no individual optimum level. The population needs different levels to have both (1) quick forgetful learning and (2) wise conservative minds.

There are harmful or disadvantageous mutations whose level may differ between races. It is impossible to make a statistical statement on their cumulative impact, however, there is a simple remedy: unrestricted inter-breeding leading to heterosis (improved functionality).

With all that variability, the brain is an optimum adapting device and that optimum is derived from the [optimality of the learn drive](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Optimality_of_the_learn_drive "Optimality of the learn drive"). The optimization criteria are modulated by genetic factors, and all forms of averaging or gauging superiority are a waste of time. It would be comparable to pondering the superiority of a chemist over a physicist.

Whatever differences emerge due to the [diversity](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Diversity "Diversity") of environments and personalities will be unmeasurable for lack of a single measurement criterion. The ecosystem of a forest survives on the strength of diversity. There is no IQ or EcoQ measure that can comprehensively compare the contribution of a wolf to a contribution of a beaver. The distribution of diversity is largely independent of the color (beyond cultural effects). In other words, diversity within color is far greater than the diversity across color boundaries.

Individual variations in passions and skills are an essential ingredient of global intelligence

## Self-esteem is a form of prevention

If you read these words, and your goal is to achieve high levels of intelligence, the color of your skin matters only as much as it will affect your obstacle course in life. Obstacles can also be seen as a challenge that leads to a different form of adaptation. However, your strategies in acquiring new knowledge will remain unchanged.

The biggest enemies of an attentive, confident, and happy mind are measurements and comparisons at school or in the workplace. The greatest minds among the discriminated (incl. women) are born in conditions that help one focus on passions with little awareness of the discrimination. That awareness can also be well counter-balanced with self-confidence.

Skin color affects intelligence largely through the ill will of others. It helps to care less

For race inequalities to be eliminated, we only need to provide children with equal access to rich environments. As self-esteem is a key component of overcoming discrimination, unschooling and free thought should play a bigger role for the less privileged as long as rich learning environments are secured.

## My guilty conscience

I was raised largely by women, and for some reason, by the time of my first vote in an election, I still thought female politicians were less smart. I probably picked up the stereotype from people surrounding me or from the media. As for racial discrimination, it was the opposite. My first great hero was Muhammad Ali. To my mind, he was part of a superior race. When my love for music exploded at the age of 14 or so, I was at first unaware of the names of my favorite performers. Only after 2-3 years I discovered that most of what I love is black. When we formed a music band with friends from Africa in Poznan, I constantly felt like an inferior species. Africans seem to be born with natural rhythms that took me a while to grasp. Their incredible self-confidence in music or at school was rooted in the fact that they came from elite circles and many came to Poland with nearly no knowledge of racism. It was a long process for me to see that my thinking about women is as dumb as the myth of black intelligence that never seemed plausible to me. Brain science helped, but my own early biases helped me understand the social mechanics of the discrimination
