[[zpkulaeffpqkcwplcpzbw6i|The mnemonic medium supplies expert-authored prompts to remove the burden of prompt-writing]], which helps ameliorate [Writing good spaced repetition memory prompts is hard](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/zKy4FsHTcf8LdkgXkMueeGL). But by including the prompts, we also help readers who want to write some prompts of their own. They get to see expert examples of prompt-writing, which they can use as templates for their own prompts.

Several [[znvvrcxnkjk7pcabibvaic1|Quantum Country]] readers have told us that they’d tried and abandoned Anki before reading Quantum Country, but that they’ve since returned to it now that they have a clearer idea of how to write prompts.
