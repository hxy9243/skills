## Big Self-Supervised Models are Strong Semi-Supervised Learners

### Key Idea

The paper proposes a three-step semi-supervised learning framework for visual data

1. unsupervised or self-supervised pretraining using a modified [[posts-chen20-simpl-framew-contr-learn-visual-repres|SimCLR]]
2. Supervised fine-tuning
3. Distillation using unlabeled data
