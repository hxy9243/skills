## 2020–2024

Minimal is an [open source](https://github.com/kepano/obsidian-minimal) distraction-free theme for [[obsidian|Obsidian]]. Minimal makes it easy to customize the appearance of Obsidian, to create your ideal writing interface.

See details and documentation at [minimal.guide](https://minimal.guide/)
