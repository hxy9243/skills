**Siesta** is a mid-day nap taken to increase evening productivity. **Siesta** is often taken [after a meal](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Optimum_diet "Optimum diet"), and is said to benefit health and longevity. The optimum time for siesta occurs 7-8 hours from natural waking. This optimum time can be visualized in [SleepChart](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/SleepChart "SleepChart") as a period during which learning performance drops for circadian reasons.

The name is actually derived from Biblical *hora sexta*, which means *sixth hour* counting from dawn (12:00-15:00 in Jewish calendar).
