[Mass mediums mostly lack an authored time dimension beyond a day](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/zBcm1RNbzedjDMTt8rRxoWt). This seems like a powerful authorial dimension to explore, as illustrated by e.g. [Guided meditation apps are naturally spread over time](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/zSNDTj75ULnYMo7pGm2Frg7).

We might call texts with an authored time dimension *timeful texts.*

- [[zcquidgcnqncwb6ptvhdrwk|The mnemonic medium keeps readers in contact with material over time]]
- [The mnemonic medium can be adapted to author an experience which unfolds over time](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/zKRrv2DmKp3VyDEp3oXrKXQ)
	- [Mnemonic texts could be serialized in small sections over time, timed to reader recall](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/zJaSxZHpWr5fq7vQtHUVuMp) (like [Execute Program’s lessons don’t unlock until you’ve successfully reviewed their prerequisites](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/zHLWyC5LVXZLWE9RmoC1gua))
