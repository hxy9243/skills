# Category-Level Market Analysis

Your goal is to aggregate the individual company profiles generated by `company_research.md` into comprehensive category-level insights.

For each business category (e.g., Base Model, Custom Silicon, Inference Platforms):

## 1. Macro-Overview
Generate a 2-3 paragraph macro-overview based strictly on the collected profiles and web search. Analyze:
- What are the macro trends affecting this category?
- Where is the capital concentration flowing?
- What is the 6-month outlook for these companies?

## 2. Competitive Pros/Cons Matrix
Synthesize the strengths and weaknesses of each company within the category into a structured Markdown table:

| Company | Key Advantage (Pro) | Primary Weakness / Risk (Con) | Target Niche |
|---|---|---|---|
| ... | ... | ... | ... |

*Example:* d-Matrix (Pro: High efficiency for inference; Con: Requires custom software stack adoption; Target: Data centers optimizing generative AI).
