## 2002

A pixel art skin for Winamp 2 with a grungy cyberpunk aesthetic. Rusty Heaven has been archived at the [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/winampskin_rustyheaven) and the [Winamp Skin Museum](https://skins.webamp.org/skin/97f0a678351490d85b71fe007b9f502d/RustyHeaven_v2.wsz).
