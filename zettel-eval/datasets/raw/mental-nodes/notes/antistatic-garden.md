"It's less than a blog, more than a tweet." As part of my exploration of [[a-gardening-guide-for-your-mind|digital gardens]] and inspired by Anna and Kelly Pendergrast, this is a little experiment to visually document interesting things I learn about every week. See [their Antistatic slides](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1GWSFltGp0Qr9YKzu4cc_4eGhFcWCrzGN0zn5G0vp3mE/mobilepresent) for reference.

While I believe [[writing-is-thinking|writing is thinking]], writing can be visual. Mind maps are such examples of attempts at [[mind-networks-modelling|mind networks modelling]].
