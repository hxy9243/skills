Innovation is combinational in nature. Sudden eureka moments are an illusion, the tip of the iceberg hiding the [[networked-thinking-can-happen-at-many-levels|network]] of existing ideas our mind slowly connected over time to generate something that feels brand new.

Progress is built [[divergent-minds-as-drivers-of-human-evolution|on the shoulders of creative connectors]] who can see patterns where others see none, who are able to simulate conversations between authors by connecting the dots across different fields, and who are part of learning communities that foster curiosity and creativity.

Maybe creative thinking should be renamed patterned thinking or networked thinking?
