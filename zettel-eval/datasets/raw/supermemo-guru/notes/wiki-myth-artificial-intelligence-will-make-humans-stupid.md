## Myth

Teachers often rebel against ChatGPT, which they see as a tool for cheating at school. They claim that [artificial intelligence](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Artificial_intelligence "Artificial intelligence") will deprive kids of their ability to think independently.

✗ Myth: **Artificial intelligence will make you lazy and stupid**

## Fact

Teachers are only partly right. Kids acquire over [100 bad habits at school](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/100_bad_habits_learned_at_school "100 bad habits learned at school"). Instead of being guided by the [learn drive](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Learn_drive "Learn drive"), they follow instructions. When doing tasks they often optimize for *"minimum expenditure of energy and time"*. Kids conditioned to be performance animals naturally seek minimum expenditure of energy. They do not capitalize on the power of AI to enhance their intellectual capacity. They get their answers and move on to other things.

It is a very different story with kids who never went to school or became [free learners](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Free_learner "Free learner") later in life. They use AI as a tool to reach new levels of [intelligence](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Intelligence "Intelligence"). They delegate jobs to AI, and operate at meta-cognitive level. They gain new insights and new powers.

People who love learning, naturally use [artificial intelligence](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Artificial_intelligence "Artificial intelligence") to learn better
