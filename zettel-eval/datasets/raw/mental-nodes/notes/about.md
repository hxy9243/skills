FAQ

*Why a digital garden?*

I jot down raw thoughts in my note-taking app, and publish longer essays on my blog. My [[a-gardening-guide-for-your-mind|digital garden]] is a space to connect early-stage ideas, collect evergreen notes, and plant the seeds for more ambitious work.

*Is there a table of content?*

No. Just click on any link within a note or a backlink in the sidebar to navigate through the content.

*How can I discuss these ideas?*

The best way to reach out is on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/). Click on the Twitter icon in the sidebar of any note and add your comment. I will be automatically tagged and can get back to you.

*I want to build a digital garden as well.*

That's a great idea. You can start with [this tutorial](https://nesslabs.com/digital-garden-tiddlywiki). There are many other ways to go about it, so feel free to tinker or try a completely different route.
