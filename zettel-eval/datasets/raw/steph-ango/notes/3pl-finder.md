## 2022

[See the directory](https://airtable.com/appTGq2PqSwtb5X8C/shr3DNBPRQyfJ5osT)

A directory of 250 third-party logistics firms (3PLs), with searchable and filterable structured data. Fulfilling products is essential to ecommerce businesses, yet finding the right fulfillment partner is difficult. As an advisor to many ecommerce entrepreneurs I’ve been frequently asked which 3PLs I recommend, so I built this tool to offer a deeper answer to the question.
