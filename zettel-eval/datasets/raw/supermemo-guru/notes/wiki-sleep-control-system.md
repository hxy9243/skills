## Description

This brainmap of the sleep control system has been compiled for [Good sleep, good learning](https://super-memory.com/articles/sleep.htm) in 2012. Some of the structures have been investigated in animals only (rats, cats, monkeys, etc.). Some of the functions are hypothetical. Expect a flood of new research that will expand and modify this picture. See references below for more details. See the source article for the description of the role of individual nuclei: [Science of sleep](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Science_of_sleep "Science of sleep").
