A collection of made-up words, invented both for fun and to describe particular experiences.

---

ENGLISH

**Mindframing.** *(n)* Actively shaping one's mind frames. "I apply mindframing to improve my productivity."

**To maze.** *(v)* To [[consciousness|consciously]] let oneself wander during the research journey. e.g. "I got this idea while mazing around."

**Noegraphy.** *(n)* The study and practice of mapping thoughts and ideas.

---

FRENCH

**Esplénitude.** *(n, f)* Pleine [[consciousness|conscience]], mindfulness. e.g. "Je pratique l'esplénitude" or "Elle a pris un cours d'esplénitude."
