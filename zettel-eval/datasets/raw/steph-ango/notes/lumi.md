## 2015–2022

I co-founded [Lumi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lumi_\(company\)) with Jesse Genet in 2015. We created a software platform to help businesses find and collaborate with factories. Lumi was acquired in 2021.

Lumi was my second company with Jesse after [[inkodye|Inkodye]]. We started Lumi to scratch our own itch after discovering how difficult it is was to build our own supply chain for Inkodye.
