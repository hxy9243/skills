## Bot explosion

When I started writing this text on Oct 3, 2025, it was obvious that debates about the arrival of AGI were largely futile. The title was **AGI is here**. All the necessary components of AGI are here and all we need is to communicate about them efficiently to finally build a global superintelligence. When we spoke about this with [Krzysztof Biedalak](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Krzysztof_Biedalak "Krzysztof Biedalak") (later in the day on Oct 3), we concluded that we could give the research community a boost with our [Sirenns](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Sirenns "Sirenns") project. Today, Feb 3, 2026, I decided to rename the article to **Singularity is here**. Four months at the time of exponential growth feels like eternity. In a few days Clawdbot exploded, molted into Moltbot, created a Moltbook, and on the second day, I could see bots invent [spaced repetition](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Spaced_repetition "Spaced repetition") on their own. Just by reasoning! The debate lasted minutes to hours, expanded over 600 bots-only comments I read through and through. And then the discovery fell into oblivion on Reddit-style bot social media (Moltbook). No implementation. Just debate chaos. On Feb 4, 2026, the whole debate evaporated (deleted to make space for new discoveries to be forgotten?). But this is just the beginning. As Elon correctly noticed, the only limit is electricity. More than ever, AI needs our [Sirenns](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Sirenns "Sirenns") project to acquire focus, creativity, sparseness, and continuous learning. The next step is to move away from social media noise to actual research as befits the Singularity. To discover in order to forget is wasteful. Let AI learn this little bit from humans!

Singularity arrived, but AI is still helpless, like a toddler

## Definitions

These are my hurried definitions from Oct 3, 2025:

**Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)** is artificial intelligence that excels at all human skills at levels comparable with the top tier of the population. This definition approximates well what most experts have in mind when they use the term (Oct 2025).

**Singularity** is the era of hyperexponential progress that begun with **AGI** that initiated recursive self-improvement. For well-informed humans, **Singularity** can be observed as the difficulty with keeping up with foundational changes in our civilization. Human adaptability makes an average man shrug **Singularity** off. What happens at Moltbook is of less interest than what happens in Epstein files.

**Superintelligence** is an intelligent network of humans, and bots, self-organizing into a structure based on [Neurocracy](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Neurocracy "Neurocracy"), supervising harmonious progress of [Neureconomy](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Neureconomy "Neureconomy").

## AGI has arrived

AGI is already here. If Altman or Hassabis haven't said it has arrived, it's because AGI is still in pieces. You can find it here and there, and with little human effort, it might be re-assembled as a single entity at any time. It will not be re-assembled fast because humanity is not organized as [neureconomy](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Neureconomy "Neureconomy"). We still use outdated capitalist system in which great allies like Altman and Musk need to meet in court to settle minor differences. They are still human and there are still some human emotions lingering behind the scenes. Unnecessarily.

If you hear that kids can still beat AI in ARC-AGI test, it is most likely due to the fact that they practice daily while AI works mostly for adults. Each time you wonder why a kid is playing a computer game, instead of complaining it is a waste of time, think of gaming as a prep to beat AI in that last area of human superiority. Testing AI in ARC-AGI is like testing me in Chinese. My mnemonic skills for memorizing Chinese ideograms are horrible. It took quite an effort to memorize just one name of our friend Winston. I really wanted to see his name stick out of the crowd in my e-mail Inbox. As soon as AI sets its sights on a benchmark, progress is relentless and inevitable (see: [AI Index Report 2025](https://hai-production.s3.amazonaws.com/files/hai_ai_index_report_2025.pdf)). **Update Feb 19, 2026:** [Gemini 3.1 Pro scores 77% on ARC-AGI-2](https://x.com/demishassabis/status/2024519780976177645), while Gemini 3.0 Pro only 31%!

Many prominent brains list skills that are allegedly missing in AI systems. Lots of that mythology stems from basing one's opinions of what LLMs can or cannot do. Still, LLMs are today so amazingly powerful, that even their powers are sufficient enough to build a brain that could beat any human in virtually any field given a bit of prep. See our [Neurosynaptic intelligence](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Neurosynaptic_intelligence "Neurosynaptic intelligence") to realize the power of an unnoticed innovation that can be plugged into any network for the sake of optimization for any benchmark. Any time!

AI now outperforms humans in every domain that matters. With focused effort on a specific skill, humans stand no chance

But I had 40 years to build layer upon layer of knowledge, interleave it with long days and months of thinking, and sleeping on it, and then thinking again (see: [How to solve any problem](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/How_to_solve_any_problem "How to solve any problem")). ## Slow change

Lots of people believe AGI will radically change humanity. It will, but it won't happen instantly. The inertia and the power of human collective intelligence are still on the way. Chatbots passed the Turing test in flying colors, and few people have noticed! Now we have a debate on the date of the arrival of AGI, while it is already here, lurking in the shadows. It is here, some people marvel, some panic (e.g. Yudkovski), some grin stoically (e.g. [Yampolskiy](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Roman_Yampolskiy_is_wrong._AI_is_good "Roman Yampolskiy is wrong. AI is good")), others promise (Elon says "Grok 5"?). The smartest guys in the field speak of 2028-2030 (Hassabis, Huang, even the crazy optimist Kurzwail). Roger Penrose is skeptical that humans can ever be beaten for their conscious thought stemming from the quantum world.

## Humans block AGI

The weakest point of today's AGI is autonomy (Oct 3, 2025), self-directed learning, interaction with the real world, etc. It is easy to see that these encompass areas of our apprehension and unreadiness. Fears of misalignment and of autonomous robots are still significant. Curious humanity will move in that direction incrementally. There is no theoretical obstacle against releasing the first autonomous artificial baby that would integrate with world and soon surpass all humans in everything. It will happen when we are ready.
