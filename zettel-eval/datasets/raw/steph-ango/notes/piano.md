## Instructions

The cheat sheet is scaled for letter-sized paper (8.5 × 11 inches), and a keyboard that has octaves 6.5 inches wide. You may need to adjust the scale of the print to match the size of your paper and keyboard.

1. Print at 100% with no scaling or margins.
2. Fold lengthwise along the middle of the sheet.
3. Trim the edges, or fold them back.
4. Place the folded paper behind the keys of your piano and slide horizontally to line it up along an octave.
