**Creative elaboration** is a process in which [creativity](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Creativity "Creativity") is used to expand knowledge by new ideas without the need for new information input. In **creative elaboration**, new knowledge is helpful, but it is not necessary. New knowledge can flow in incrementally, and provide new insight into the elaborated [semantic framework](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Semantic_framework "Semantic framework").

**Creative elaboration** occurs all the time in the thinking brain, and in sleep. It is a vital component of the [conceptualization](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Conceptualization "Conceptualization") process.

**Creative elaboration** may be documented in the process of [incremental reading](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Incremental_reading "Incremental reading") where it may have a form of notes and annotations added to existing elements, or added as independent entities.

The power of **creative elaboration** is best expressed in its ultimate re-usable form: [incremental writing](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Incremental_writing "Incremental writing"). All my texts in the new millennium were written incrementally within the framework of [incremental reading](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Incremental_reading "Incremental reading"). [SuperMemo Guru](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/SuperMemo_Guru "SuperMemo Guru") (this site), takes this process to the web. All ideas are subject to **creative elaboration** in [incremental writing](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Incremental_writing "Incremental writing"), transferred to this wiki site, and continually reviewed and improved.

Wikipedia can be seen as a form of collective creative elaboration. However, the process is hobbled by the fact that Wikipedia knowledge is not coherently stored in the brain. Portions of its skeletal image are stored in the collective brain of Wikipedians. The ultimate power of Wikipedia will show when its semantics are encoded as a [concept network](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Concept_network "Concept network"). Once that network implements [conceptual computation](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Conceptual_computation "Conceptual computation"), we will arrive at a form of [artificial intelligence](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Artificial_intelligence "Artificial intelligence").

[Georgios Zonnios](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Georgios_Zonnios "Georgios Zonnios") was first to use the term **creative elaboration** in January 2007 in reference to his own way of working with [incremental reading](https://supermemo.guru/wiki/Incremental_reading "Incremental reading").
