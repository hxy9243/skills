Many [[taking-raw-notes-is-useless|note-takers]] hoard content because it gives them an illusion of knowledge. The best way to truly understand anything is to use the generation effect: to [[a-gardening-guide-for-your-mind|produce your own content]], in your own words, and to [[the-only-way-to-learn-in-public-is-to-build-in-public|share it with the world]] to create a feedback loop.

- **Collect.** Note-taking, raw snippets, Kindle highlights + personal commentary.
- **Connect.** Link to previous notes. [[do-not-keep-orphan-notes|Do not keep orphan notes]].
- **Create.** Write original article.
