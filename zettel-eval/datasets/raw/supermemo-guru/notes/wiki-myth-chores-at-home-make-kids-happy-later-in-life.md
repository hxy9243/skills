I had no chores at home as a kid. On occasion, I did some in return for pocket money. Chores that become "compulsory", I see as form of abuse. The prime "duty" of children is to develop and grow intelligence!

As always in sloppy research, correlations are abused. Chores are not different from learning. Coercive learning undermines mental health, while voluntary learning is the most sustainable source of joy in life. Chores might have a similar effect as productivity is essential for "eudaimonic well-being". Chores imposed coercively may have the opposite effect. They turn a household into a labor camp.
